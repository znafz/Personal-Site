About Me

My name is Zachary Nafziger.

I live in Lititz, PA, and I have passions for both computer science and music.

Computer Science

I am currently a senior at Grove City College, where I am majoring in Computer Science. I have an overall GPA of 3.19 and a major GPA of 3.53. In addition to my education, I have done some learning on my own. A few technical skills that I have researched outside of school are Web Design, Graphic Design, Data Science, and a number of languages, such as Python, R, etc.

Music

One of my favorite side hobbies is music production and performance. Although I have no formal training, I'm self-taught in guitar, singing, and music production. I've been writing my own music since late 2010, and one of my early productions has more than 3.2 million hits. Since this, I've added vocals to my songs and improved tremendously in my songwriting, mixing, and mastering capabilities.

Education

In my time at Grove City, I have learned a number of valuable skills. I have primarily used C++ and I've gained considerable knowledge in Object Oriented Programming. I have also taken classes in Database Management, Introductory Computer Graphics, and Game Design & Development, to name a few. In addition to these technical skills, I've seen improvements in my mastery of writing, public speaking, and general communication, and have even found myself enjoying those aspects.

I've found that as my classes have increased in difficulty, my desire to excel has grown, and my grades reflect that. The pressure of more difficult projects hasn't deterred my love of computer science, but rather has increased it. I enjoy being challenged and creating new solutions to complex problems.

Another major part of my experience at Grove City is my on-campus involvement. During my time I've been involved in a few on-campus organizations and events. I am a member of the Adelphikos fraternity. As a group, we seek to be men of honor and men of courage. The fraternity is very active on campus and seeks to promote unity between the Greek and independent communities through outreach, social activities, and a Christ-centered lifestyle. I've also been a part of CRU, where I have served as the Weekly Meeting Leader, along with helping to lead worship.

Experience
